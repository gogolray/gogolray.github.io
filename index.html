About

Scholar · Poet · Visual Ethnographer

I am Assistant Professor of English at Amity University Kolkata. My work develops a framework of planetary cosmopolitics extending Bruno Latour's Earthbound into a distributed relational ontology attentive to fiction, climate change, and nonhuman articulation.
