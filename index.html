About

Scholar · Poet · Visual Ethnographer

I am an Assistant Professor of English at Amity Institute of English Studies and Research, where I teach Business Communication, Drama, Literary Theory, and Modern Indian English Literature.


My work develops a framework of planetary cosmopolitics extending Bruno Latour's Earthbound into a distributed relational ontology attentive to fiction, climate change, and nonhuman articulation.


My work draws on thinkers including Isabelle Stengers, Donna Haraway, Vandana Singh, Rob Nixon, and Amitav Ghosh, integrating Whitehead's process philosophy with environmental humanities and relational ontology. I treat fiction not as illustration of theory but as its very ground.
