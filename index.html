About

I am Assistant Professor of English at Amity University Kolkata. My work engages planetary cosmopolitics, environmental humanities, and science studies, drawing on thinkers such as Bruno Latour and contemporary climate fiction.

  • Posthumanism
  • Dalit Feminist Studies
