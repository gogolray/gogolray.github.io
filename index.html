About

I am Assistant Professor of English at Amity University Kolkata. My research develops a framework of planetary cosmopolitics at the intersection of environmental humanities, science studies, and literary theory, extending Latourian approaches to climate fiction and Anthropocene thought.

Research Interests

Planetary Cosmopolitics; Environmental Humanities; Climate Fiction (Cli-Fi); Actor-Network Theory; Posthumanism; Dalit Feminist Studies.
