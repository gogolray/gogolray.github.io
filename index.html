Assistant Professor | Literature & Planetary Cosmopolitics

I study how literature participates in the making of planetary realities. My work brings together Bruno Latour, Whitehead, and science studies to rethink agency, temporality, and nonhuman beinghood.

About

My research develops a framework of planetary cosmopolitics that extends the Earthbound into a distributed relational ontology. I approach literature not as representation, but as an active participant in world-making.
